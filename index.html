Pomodoro Clock

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management technique used to break down work into intervals separated by short breaks. This is believed to help you stay focused on your work for longer periods of time. The Pomodoro Clock helps with setting the intervals and keeping track of the number of intervals completed.
